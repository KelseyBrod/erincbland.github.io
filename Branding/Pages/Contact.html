Welcome to Cheerio!, a fun new social media music sign. The main focus of the site should be the social aspect of listening to music. This grid is supposed to represent your friends that have different icons and you can see what they're listening to in real time. It would also be cool if the icons had a "sleep mode" or something similar so you can see when your friends are active versus when they aren't. Ideally, you'd also be able to comment on what they're listening to.
